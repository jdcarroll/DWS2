Player Evaluation:

players are evaluated by position on hitting, fielding, throwing. Players are allowed to choose a primary & secondary position. (Example: SS/P, 3B/C or OF/P) Evaluations are performed for 2 reasons, to find out where the player is currently as well as for college recruiting purposes. Younger players also receive a projected score on each area of evaluation. This number will indicate where the player will be by the start of his senior year. Once player is a sophomore in HS these evaluations can be used for college recruitment.

Physical Norm Testing:

inclusive of, height weight, body composition, leg power, speed, agility, arm strength, core power, bat speed and grip strength. Results are given to parent/player and program is designed for advancement. Once player is a sophomore in HS these evaluations can be used for college recruitment.

Circuit X-treme:

a highly disciplined training circuit of hitting, fielding, throwing, lower half power, core power, endurance, speed, agility & other extreme performance drills designed to maximize repetition create perfect mechanics in a short amount of time. (for all ages & levels)

Circuit X-treme Advanced:

same as Circuit X-treme but at a whole other level! (for advanced players only)
